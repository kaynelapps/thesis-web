Cookie Policy

Last updated: January 2024

1. What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your computer or mobile device when you visit a website. They help websites remember information about your visit, making it easier to visit the site again and making the site more useful to you.

2. How We Use Cookies

Thesis Generator uses cookies to:

  • Remember your preferences and settings
  • Keep you signed in to your account
  • Analyze how you use our website
  • Improve our service performance
  • Provide personalized content and features
  • Measure the effectiveness of our marketing campaigns

3. Types of Cookies We Use

Essential Cookies

These cookies are necessary for the website to function properly. They include:

  • Authentication cookies to keep you logged in
  • Security cookies to protect against fraud
  • Load balancing cookies to distribute traffic

Performance Cookies

These cookies help us understand how visitors interact with our website:

  • Google Analytics cookies for usage statistics
  • Performance monitoring cookies
  • Error tracking cookies

Functional Cookies

These cookies enable enhanced functionality and personalization:

  • Language preference cookies
  • Theme and display preference cookies
  • Feature usage tracking cookies

Marketing Cookies

These cookies are used to deliver relevant advertisements:

  • Google Ads cookies for targeted advertising
  • Social media integration cookies
  • Campaign tracking cookies

4. Third-Party Cookies

We use several third-party services that may set cookies:

Google Services

  • Google Analytics: Tracks website usage and performance
  • Google Ads: Delivers targeted advertisements
  • Firebase: Provides backend services and analytics

Social Media

  • Facebook: Social sharing and advertising
  • Twitter: Social sharing features

Other Services

  • AppsFlyer: Mobile app attribution and analytics
  • Hotjar: User behavior analytics (if applicable)

5. Managing Your Cookie Preferences

Browser Settings

You can control cookies through your browser settings:

  • Chrome: Settings > Privacy and Security > Cookies
  • Firefox: Preferences > Privacy & Security > Cookies
  • Safari: Preferences > Privacy > Cookies
  • Edge: Settings > Privacy > Cookies

Opt-Out Options

You can opt out of specific tracking:

  • Google Analytics Opt-out
  • Google Ads Settings
  • Facebook Ad Preferences

6. Cookie Consent

When you first visit our website, we'll ask for your consent to use cookies. You can:

  • Accept all cookies
  • Reject non-essential cookies
  • Customize your cookie preferences
  • Change your preferences at any time

7. Impact of Disabling Cookies

If you disable cookies, some features may not work properly:

  • You may need to log in repeatedly
  • Your preferences won't be saved
  • Some personalized features may be unavailable
  • Website performance may be affected

8. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for legal reasons. We'll notify you of any significant changes by posting the updated policy on our website.
